# Methodology — EV Market Expansion

### Step 1 — Define Objectives  
Understand the goal: validate Tier-2 potential, map adoption levers, build actionable GTM.

### Step 2 — Primary Research  
74 user interviews across 3 cities:
- Students (22)  
- Delivery riders (12)  
- Mechanics (18)  
- Office employees (10)  

Collected insights on cost barriers, charging habits, and service expectations.

### Step 3 — Secondary Research  
Policy review (FAME-II, UP EV Policy), competitor pricing, infrastructure availability.

### Step 4 — Financial Modelling  
Used top-down approach: total 2W owners × expected adoption × avg price.  
Ran sensitivity analysis for adoption (10–20%).

### Step 5 — Strategy Drafting  
Built phased rollout roadmap and partnership plan.  
Validated plan with local partner feedback.
